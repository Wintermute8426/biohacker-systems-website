1. Introduction

Biohacker Systems ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Biohacker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App, particularly your Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

HIPAA COMPLIANCE

We are committed to maintaining compliance with HIPAA regulations and protecting your health information with the highest security standards.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Account credentials (encrypted password hash)
  • Profile preferences

2.2 Health Data (Protected Health Information)

  • Laboratory test results (blood work, biomarkers, metabolic panels)
  • Peptide protocols and dosing schedules
  • Cycle tracking information (start/end dates, protocol details)
  • Health metrics and biomarker trends
  • Research notes and personal health observations

2.3 Device Information

  • Device identifiers (for biometric authentication)
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Biometric authentication status (fingerprint/Face ID enabled)

2.4 Usage Analytics (Optional)

  • App feature usage patterns
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Error logs and crash reports (de-identified)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively for:

  • Health Tracking: Display and manage your lab results, peptide protocols, and cycle tracking
  • Protocol Management: Calculate dosages, track adherence, and manage protocol schedules
  • Data Visualization: Generate charts and insights from your biomarker trends
  • Account Security: Authenticate your identity and protect your account
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality (anonymized data only)
  • Research Library: Provide personalized research recommendations based on your protocols

4. How We Protect Your Information

4.1 Encryption

  • At Rest: All health data encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • In Transit: TLS 1.3 for all network communications
  • Database: Row-level security enforced by Supabase

4.2 Authentication & Access Control

  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint/Face ID)
  • Automatic session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity
  • Multi-factor authentication (planned)

4.3 HIPAA Safeguards

  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with all third-party processors
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access logging and monitoring
  • Employee training on HIPAA compliance
  • Incident response procedures

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share your information only with HIPAA-compliant service providers:

  • Supabase (Database & Authentication): Stores encrypted health data under a signed BAA
  • Google Cloud Platform (Backend Services): Hosts AI analysis services under a signed BAA
  • Analytics Providers (If Enabled): De-identified usage data only; no PHI shared

5.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal or health information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information when required by law:

  • Court orders or subpoenas
  • Legal investigations
  • Protection of our rights or safety of others
  • Regulatory compliance (e.g., HHS audits)

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access

  • View all your health data within the App
  • Request a complete export of your data in machine-readable format (JSON/PDF)

6.2 Modify

  • Edit or update any information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate data

6.3 Delete

  • Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Data deletion is permanent and irreversible
  • Deletion requests processed within 30 days

6.4 Export

  • Download your complete health history
  • Receive data in portable formats (JSON, CSV, PDF)

6.5 Restrict Processing

  • Opt out of optional analytics
  • Disable AI insights features

6.6 HIPAA Rights

  • Receive a copy of this Notice of Privacy Practices
  • Request an accounting of disclosures
  • Request restrictions on certain uses and disclosures
  • Request confidential communications

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained indefinitely while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Accounts inactive for 3+ years will receive deletion notice
  • Deleted Accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backups: Backup copies purged within 90 days of deletion request
  • Legal Holds: Data preserved when required by law or litigation

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover a minor has created an account, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Users

Our services are hosted in the United States. If you access the App from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and stored in the US. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via:

  • In-app notification

  • Updated "Last Modified" date

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
